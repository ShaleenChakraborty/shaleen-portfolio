About Me

I’m Shaleen Chakraborty, currently pursuing a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication at Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Pune. I am passionate about crafting scalable, intuitive, and responsive digital experiences. With a strong command over HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Spring Boot, and PostgreSQL, I enjoy building end-to-end web systems that solve real-world problems.

During my internship at Indian Railways (Signal and Telecommunication Department, Moradabad Division), I independently developed a full-stack internal portal that streamlined data recording and analysis. I designed responsive pages using HTML5, CSS, Thymeleaf, and Spring Boot, integrating Google Sheets and databases for real-time synchronization. The system is actively used by railway engineers.

I’ve also worked on cutting-edge AI/ML research in nanotechnology, presenting papers at international conferences in Malaysia and South Korea. I hold leadership roles in my college’s Innovation Council and Cultural Committee, where I’ve guided over 10+ projects and coordinated major technical-cultural fests.

Some of my key projects include:

  • Railway Entry Portal: Full-stack website with form management, user access control, Google Sheets API, and analytics.
  • Electrospinning Optimizer: ML-based system predicting nanofiber diameters with 99.89% accuracy.
  • Age & Gender Detection System: Real-time ML model integrated with a responsive web UI.
